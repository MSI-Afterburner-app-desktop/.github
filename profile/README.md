## 🚀 Overview
MSI Afterburner for Windows is a free graphics card utility that blends thorough hardware monitoring with precise control over your GPU. It supports cards from any brand — not just MSI — and lets you set core and memory clocks, power limits, and fan curves to reach the balance of speed and temperature you want. The built-in monitor keeps live tabs on GPU load, clock speeds, and CPU temp, while the customizable overlay shows an FPS counter and hardware readouts inside your games. A single MSI Afterburner download gives gamers and enthusiasts one place to measure, adjust, and keep watch over their hardware.

## ✨ Features
- **Live Monitoring** — Track GPU and CPU usage, clock speeds, and temperatures in real time.
- **In-Game FPS Overlay** — Display an FPS counter with GPU load and CPU temp while you game.
- **Graphics Card Tuning** — Adjust core clock, memory clock, and power limits to fit your setup.
- **Custom Fan Curves** — Tailor fan speeds to keep cooling strong and noise low.
- **Profiles & Hotkeys** — Store several configurations and switch between them in a keystroke.
- **Benchmark & Recording** — Measure performance and capture gameplay with the built-in tools.

## 🎯 Getting Started
1. Open MSI Afterburner and view the dashboard with your GPU's clocks, temperature, and usage.
2. Enable the overlay so your FPS counter and temps appear inside your games.
3. Change the core clock, memory clock, or fan curve a little at a time, applying each adjustment.
4. Play a game or run a benchmark to watch the temperatures and confirm stability.
5. Save the setup you like best as a profile and assign it to a hotkey for quick access.
